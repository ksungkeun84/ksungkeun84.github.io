I am a Ph.D. student at Texas A&M University, working with Professor Eun Jung Kim. My research area is computer architecture and systems. Specifically, I focus on computer architecture support for security and network-on-chip (NoC). Prior to my Ph.D. program, I worked for Samsung Electronics and developed Android Smartphones.
